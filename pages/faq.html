Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Questions Answer
How can I place an order? To place an order, simply browse our products, add your desired items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your details and complete the payment to confirm your order.
What are the available payment methods? We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and digital wallets like Google Pay or Apple Pay.
How long does shipping take? Shipping usually takes 3–7 business days, depending on your location. For more details, you can check the estimated delivery time at checkout.
What is your return policy? We offer a 30-day return policy for unused and unopened products. If you need to return an item, please contact our customer service for assistance.
Do you offer warranties for your products? Yes, most of our products come with a 1-year manufacturer’s warranty. Please refer to the product description for warranty details or contact us for more information.
